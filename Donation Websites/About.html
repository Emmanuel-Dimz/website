About Us

AIMS AND OBJECTIVES

➤ To enjoin what is right and forbid what is wrong and to faster the true practices and beliefs of Islam.

➤ To promote the religious , social , spiritual and educational advancement of the community.

➤ To coordinate community development projects in communities with our population.

➤ To provide finance to enhance the experience of Muslims in pursuing various occupation in our community......

➤ To provide humanitarian , social and welfare aid and / or assistance to the most needy and deprived Muslim people of our community.

➤ To erect , maintain and assist in the administration of Masjids.

➤ To build and assist in the building and administering of madressas in various localities and to provide therein instruction of Islamic shariah according to the Sunni school of thought as defined in the preamble.

➤ To maintain and assist in the upkeep of Muslim cemeteries.

➤ The society is wholly charitable , non - profit making.

➤ To peacefully co - exist with other diverse communities in Zambia.

➤ To manage and supervise the burial of deceased persons within the Muslim community.

➤ Propagating Islam throughout Zambia.

➤ Establishing, running schools and Islamic education centers.

➤ Establishing Mosques.

➤ Assisting the needy.
